Single Leg Stand (Sideways)

Instructions

  • Stand upright with your feet hip-width apart.
  • If needed, stand near a wall or chair for support.
  • Slowly lift one leg out to the side while keeping your torso upright.
  • Hold the position for a few seconds while maintaining balance.
  • Return to the starting position smoothly.
  • Repeat for the prescribed duration.

Reference Video

Safety Notes

  • Ensure you have a stable surface or support nearby.
  • Maintain a comfortable range of motion.
  • Stop if you experience any pain or discomfort.
  • Keep your movements slow and controlled.
